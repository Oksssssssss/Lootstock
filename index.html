📌 How buying works
  1. Add items to your cart (set quantity if you want more than one).
  2. Open the cart and hit Checkout — it opens PayPal with your total.
  3. Complete your payment there and save the confirmation.
  4. Go to Discord and send a direct message to itz.zan.
  5. Send your proof of payment and tell us which item(s) you got.
  6. If the account is offline, delivery may take longer than the usual 10–15 minutes.
  7. Once everything's verified, you'll receive your item. Thanks for the support! 🙌
💵 Note: All prices are listed in Canadian Dollars (CAD). If you're paying from another country, your bank or PayPal may apply a currency conversion.

FAQ

How long does delivery take?

Usually 10–15 minutes after payment is confirmed. If it's taking longer than that, we may be offline — hang tight, we'll get back to you.

How do I receive my order?

Send your PayPal payment confirmation over on Discord and tell us which item(s) you bought. Once it's verified, delivery follows. Keep that confirmation handy in case we need to check it again.

What if I don't receive my order?

Message us on Discord with your payment confirmation and we'll sort it out. If we genuinely can't deliver, you're covered by the Refund Policy below (no payment = no refund, obviously).

Do monthly plans auto-renew?

No — you'll need to renew manually each period. You can cancel any time by messaging us on Discord before your next billing date.

What payment methods do you accept?

PayPal only, prices in CAD. Your bank or PayPal may apply conversion if you're paying from elsewhere.

Terms & Policies

Terms of Service

By purchasing, you agree to these terms. Orders are paid through PayPal and fulfilled through Discord. It's on you to send accurate payment info and keep your confirmation until delivery. We can refuse or cancel any order at our discretion — a full refund follows if money was sent.

Refund Policy

  • Not delivered within a reasonable time after payment? You're entitled to a full refund — message us on Discord with your confirmation.
  • No refunds just for changing your mind once an order's already delivered.
  • To request one, message us on Discord with your confirmation and the reason. We aim to respond quickly.

Rental Rules

Monthly plans grant access for the billing period you paid for. Cancel any time before the next billing date to avoid another charge — cancelling doesn't refund the current period.

Delivery Times

Typically 10–15 minutes after payment confirmation. Delayed longer than that? Message us on Discord and we'll resolve it or refund you.

Privacy

We only collect what's needed to fulfill orders: your PayPal confirmation and Discord username. Used solely to verify and deliver — never sold or shared.
