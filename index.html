Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Orders are delivered shortly after payment is confirmed. (10-15 mins is the fastest) Most are handled within the same day — if your order is taking longer than expected, we may be offline and will get back to you soon.

How do I receive my order?

Once your payment confirmation is sent over to us, we will verify and make sure we got the money and will send over the paid purchased. delivery take up to 10-15 minutes. MAKE SURE to Keep your payment confirmation handy in case we need to verify it again..

What if I don't receive my order?

Message us on Discord with your payment confirmation and we'll sort it out. If we can't deliver what you paid for, you're entitled to a refund under our Refund Policy below. (if no payment was given we will not give a refund)

Can I get a refund?

Yes, in the situations where we can't get you you're paid purchased your covered by our Refund Policy. If an order wasn't delivered or wasn't as described. See the Terms & Policies section for the full details.

Do monthly plans auto-renew?

No. Monthly plans DO NOT renew automatically each month you will have to 1: Cancel the order or 2: Renew your order. Reminder that you can cancel any time by messaging us on Discord before your next billing date.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments are processed through PayPal. All prices are listed in Canadian Dollars (CAD); your bank or PayPal may apply currency conversion if you're paying from another country.

Terms & Policies

Terms of Service

By purchasing from Loopstock, you agree to these terms. Orders are paid through PayPal and fulfilled by our team through Discord. It's your responsibility to provide accurate payment information and to keep your payment confirmation until your order is delivered. We reserve the right to refuse or cancel any order at our discretion, in which case a full refund will be issued. if money was sent.

Refund Policy

  • If your order isn't delivered within a reasonable time after payment is confirmed, you're entitled to a full refund — contact us on Discord with your payment confirmation.
  • Refunds are not offered simply for changing your mind after an order has already been delivered.
  • To request a refund, message us on Discord with your payment confirmation and the reason for the request. We aim to respond promptly.

Rental Rules

Monthly plans grant access for the length of the billing period you've paid for. You can cancel any time before your next billing date to avoid being charged again; cancelling doesn't refund the current period already paid for.

Purchase Rules

One-time purchases are a single transaction covering one item — there's no recurring charge. What's included with each item is described on its product card; if anything is unclear, ask before you buy.

Delivery Times

Orders are typically delivered shortly after payment is confirmed(10-15 mins). If delivery is delayed beyond a reasonable time, contact us on Discord and we'll either resolve it or issue a refund. (if the discord account we use is not online please wait for it to go online.)

Privacy Policy

We collect only what's needed to fulfill your order: your PayPal payment confirmation and your Discord username. This information is used solely to verify and deliver your order and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Contact

For order questions, delivery issues, or refund requests, message us on Discord: itz.zan.
